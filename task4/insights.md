# Dashboard Insights

## Business Insights

- The Superstore generated a total sales revenue of **2.30M**.
- The total profit earned was **286.40K**.
- A total quantity of **38K** products was sold.
- The **Technology** category recorded the highest sales among all product categories.
- The **West** region achieved the highest sales performance.
- The **Consumer** segment contributed the largest share of overall sales.
- **Office Supplies** and **Technology** generated higher profits than **Furniture**.
- The interactive Region slicer allows users to filter the dashboard and analyze regional performance.
- The Sales Trend chart helps identify changes in sales over time for better business analysis.

## Conclusion

The dashboard provides an interactive and easy-to-understand overview of sales performance. It enables users to monitor key metrics, compare regional and category-wise performance, and gain valuable insights to support business decision-making.
