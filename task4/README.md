# Task 4 - Dashboard Design

## Objective

Design an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze Superstore sales data and present meaningful business insights using key performance indicators (KPIs) and visualizations.

---

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Power BI
- Sample Superstore Dataset (CSV)

---

## Dashboard Features

- KPI Card for Total Sales
- KPI Card for Total Profit
- KPI Card for Total Quantity
- Sales Trend Analysis (Sales by Order Date)
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Region
- Sales by Segment (Pie Chart)
- Profit by Category
- Interactive Region Slicer for filtering dashboard visuals

---

## Key Insights

- Total Sales: **2.30M**
- Total Profit: **286.40K**
- Total Quantity Sold: **38K**
- Technology generated the highest sales among all categories.
- The West region recorded the highest sales.
- The Consumer segment contributed the largest share of total sales.
- Office Supplies and Technology generated higher profits than Furniture.
