# Business Insights

## Key Findings

- Total Sales: **2.30M**
- Total Profit: **286.40K**
- Total Quantity Sold: **38K**

### Insights

- Technology generated the highest sales among all product categories.
- The West region recorded the highest overall sales.
- The Consumer segment contributed the largest share of total sales.
- Profit varied across product categories, indicating differences in product profitability.

## Conclusion

The dashboard provides a clear overview of sales performance, regional distribution, customer segments, and product category profitability, helping support data-driven business decisions.
